SUNWAY GROUP SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAMME

Overview
The Sunway Group Scholarship Programme is awarded to outstanding students who demonstrate academic excellence, strong leadership qualities, and community involvement. The scholarship supports students pursuing undergraduate studies at Sunway University and Sunway College.

Types of Scholarships

1. Sunway Full Merit Scholarship
   - Covers: Full tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly allowance of RM 500
   - Eligibility: Minimum CGPA of 3.9 in SPM or equivalent (minimum 9A+), OR STPM with minimum 4A
   - Bond: Recipients must serve a 2-year bond with Sunway Group upon graduation
   - Application period: March to May each year

2. Sunway Half Merit Scholarship
   - Covers: 50% tuition fee waiver
   - Eligibility: Minimum CGPA of 3.7 in SPM (minimum 7A in SPM), OR STPM with minimum 3A
   - No bond requirement
   - Renewable each semester subject to maintaining a CGPA of 3.5 and above

3. Sunway Sports Excellence Scholarship
   - Covers: Full tuition fees
   - Eligibility: National-level athlete representing Malaysia in recognised sports
   - Supporting documents: Official national selection letter required
   - Conditions: Must continue active participation in the sport throughout the study period

4. Sunway Community Leaders Scholarship
   - Covers: 30% tuition fee waiver
   - Eligibility: Students with outstanding community service records (minimum 200 hours documented)
   - Preference given to students from rural or underprivileged backgrounds

Eligible Programmes
All scholarship types above apply to programmes at Sunway University including:
- Foundation in Arts / Foundation in Science
- Bachelor of Business Administration
- Bachelor of Computer Science
- Bachelor of Engineering (Hons)
- Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS)
- Bachelor of Pharmacy

Application Requirements
- Completed online application form
- Certified copies of academic transcripts and certificates
- Two referee letters (one from a teacher or lecturer, one from a community leader)
- A personal statement (500-800 words) explaining your goals and why you deserve the scholarship
- Proof of extracurricular achievements (certificates, awards, photographs)

Selection Process
1. Paper screening based on academic results and supporting documents
2. Written assessment (essay and aptitude test)
3. Panel interview with Sunway Group representatives
