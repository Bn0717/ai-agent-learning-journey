MALAYSIAN GOVERNMENT SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAMMES

1. JPA (Public Service Department) Scholarship

Overview
The Jabatan Perkhidmatan Awam (JPA) scholarship is one of Malaysia's most prestigious government scholarships. It funds outstanding students for undergraduate and postgraduate studies at local and overseas universities.

Categories
a) Overseas Scholarship (Biasiswa JPA Luar Negara)
   - Destination countries: UK, USA, Australia, Japan, Germany, and others
   - Covers: Tuition fees, living allowance, airfare, books allowance, and medical insurance
   - Eligibility: SPM with a minimum of 9A+ and strong co-curricular achievements
   - Bond: 7 years of service in the Malaysian civil service after graduation

b) Local Scholarship (Biasiswa JPA Tempatan)
   - Universities: Public universities (Universiti Malaya, UTM, UPM, etc.)
   - Covers: Full tuition fees and monthly allowance of RM 800
   - Eligibility: SPM with minimum 8A, or STPM with minimum 3.5 CGPA
   - Bond: 5 years of service in the Malaysian civil service

Application Window: Opens in February/March each year at www.spa.gov.my

2. MARA Scholarship

Overview
Majlis Amanah Rakyat (MARA) scholarships support Bumiputera students to pursue higher education locally and abroad.

Types
a) Biasiswa Kecemerlangan MARA (BKM)
   - For top Bumiputera SPM graduates
   - Covers overseas study at partner universities in the UK, Australia, and New Zealand
   - Full sponsorship including tuition, living allowance, and return airflight

b) Pinjaman Biasiswa MARA
   - Loan-scholarship hybrid: converts to full scholarship if CGPA ≥ 3.5 maintained throughout studies
   - Available for diploma and degree programmes at MARA institutions and selected private universities

Eligibility (General)
   - Malaysian Bumiputera citizen
   - SPM minimum: 5A for local, 7A for overseas
   - Age: Below 25 for undergraduate programmes
   - No criminal record

3. Khazanah Scholarship (Yayasan Khazanah)

Overview
Yayasan Khazanah awards scholarships to talented Malaysians for studies at top universities worldwide.

Watan Programme (Needs-Based)
   - For students from households earning less than RM 5,000 per month
   - Destinations: Top 30 ranked universities globally
   - Covers: Full tuition, subsistence allowance, flights, and book allowance
   - Bond: 3 years of working in Malaysia's national interest sectors after graduation

Global Programme (Merit-Based)
   - Open to all Malaysians regardless of financial background
   - Requires exceptional academic record (straight A+ in SPM) and leadership portfolio
   - Includes mentoring by Khazanah's leadership team

Application: Via Yayasan Khazanah website, typically opens in November/December for the following academic year.

4. Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) Scholarship

Overview
Bank Negara Malaysia offers scholarships to students pursuing economics, finance, statistics, actuarial science, or computer science.

Coverage
   - Full tuition fees
   - Monthly living allowance of RM 1,500 (local) or equivalent in foreign currency (overseas)
   - Laptop and book allowance
   - Internship placement at Bank Negara Malaysia

Bond: 5 years of employment at Bank Negara Malaysia after graduation

Eligibility
   - SPM minimum 9A or equivalent
   - Strong interest in economics, finance, or technology
   - Excellent command of English

5. Petronas Scholarship

Overview
PETRONAS Education Scholarship Programme supports students studying in STEM fields, particularly engineering, technology, and science.

Categories
a) Degree Programme (Local)
   - Partner universities: Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS (UTP) and other Malaysian public universities
   - Covers full tuition and RM 750/month allowance

b) Degree Programme (Overseas)
   - Destinations: Australia, UK, USA, Japan
   - Full sponsorship for engineering and science disciplines

Bond: 7 years of service with PETRONAS or its subsidiary companies

Eligibility
   - SPM minimum 8A+ or STPM minimum 3.67 CGPA
   - Malaysian citizen
   - Preference for Engineering, Computer Science, Petroleum Studies, and related fields

Application Period: January to March each year at www.petronas.com/careers/scholarships
